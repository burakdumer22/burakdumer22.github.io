Blastic Bubbles

The Grand Bazaar

Pop colorful bubbles, unwrap hidden treasures, and help Baba renovate his dream house in this Ottoman-themed puzzle adventure set in Istanbul's Grand Bazaar.

🍎 App Store ↗ ▶ Google Play ↗
🫧

Pop & Chain

Tap matching bubbles to pop chains. Bigger chains mean bigger rewards — and unlock powerful special pieces like the Ottoman Cannon and Fez Bomb.

🎁

Unwrap Hidden Items

Every level hides a mystery item wrapped in bubble wrap. Pop bubbles to reveal it — from Turkish tea sets to ornate lamps and musical instruments.

🏠

Renovate the Dream House

Earn stars to upgrade Baba's family home room by room. Choose furniture, paint walls, and transform a fixer-upper into an Ottoman palace.

🧿

Ottoman Special Pieces

Create chain combos to spawn the Nazar Eye, Whirling Dervish, Sultan's Firework, and more. Combine specials for massive explosive chain reactions.

🧩

Strategic Obstacles

Break through Turkish Ice, smash Ottoman Crates, unroll Kilim Curtains, and spread magical carpets across hundreds of hand-crafted levels.

Meet the Characters

👨

Baba

A cheerful Ottoman shopkeeper with a magnificent mustache and a red Fez. He left the shop to watch "the match of the century" — now he needs your help to sort through the deliveries!

👶

Baby

Baba's adorable toddler with a tiny drawn-on mustache and an oversized Fez. Fearless, curious, and ready to pop every bubble in sight.
