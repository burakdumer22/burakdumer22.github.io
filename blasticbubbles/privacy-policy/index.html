Privacy Policy

Blastic Bubbles: The Grand Bazaar
Effective date: March 21, 2026

Hasan Burak Dümer ("we," "our," or "us") built Blastic Bubbles: The Grand Bazaar ("the App") as a free-to-play mobile game with optional in-app purchases. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

We designed the App with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • Game progress data (level completion, stars earned, coins, power-up inventory) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No account creation is required. You do not need to provide a name, email address, or any personal details to play.
  • No photos, contacts, or location data are accessed or collected.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information independently:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying interstitial and rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for processing in-app purchases (Remove Ads, coin packs). Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

Advertising

The App displays ads via Google AdMob:

  • Interstitial ads appear between levels (approximately every 2 levels).
  • Rewarded ads are optional — you choose to watch them in exchange for in-game rewards (extra moves, power-ups).
  • You may purchase a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase ($2.99) to permanently remove interstitial ads. Rewarded ads remain available as an optional choice.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App does not require any personal data to play. Ads served to users under 13 comply with applicable children's advertising regulations (COPPA).

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. We do not operate any servers that receive or store your data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. To manage data collected by third-party ad providers, you can:

  • Adjust ad tracking preferences in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Purchase the "Remove Ads" option to eliminate interstitial ad display.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
