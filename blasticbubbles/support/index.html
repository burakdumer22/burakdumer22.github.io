Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?
Go to the in-game Shop and purchase "Remove Ads" for $2.99. This permanently removes interstitial ads (the ads that appear between levels). Rewarded ads — the ones you choose to watch for extra moves or power-ups — remain available as an optional bonus.
I purchased "Remove Ads" but ads are still showing. What do I do?
Try restoring your purchase: go to the Shop screen and tap "Restore Purchases." If ads still appear, please restart the app. If the issue persists, contact us with your purchase receipt and we'll help resolve it.
How do I save my progress?
Your game progress is automatically saved to your device. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost. We're working on adding cloud save in a future update.
What are special pieces and how do I create them?
Special pieces are created by making large chains of same-color bubbles. A chain of 5 in a straight line creates an Ottoman Cannon (clears a row or column). An L-shaped or T-shaped chain of 5+ creates a Fez Bomb (clears a 3×3 area). A chain of 7+ creates a Nazar Eye (clears all of one color). Combine two special pieces for even more powerful effects!
What do the power-ups do?
There are 4 power-ups available during gameplay: the Hammer (pops any single bubble), Shuffle (rearranges the board), Color Bomb (removes all bubbles of one color), and Hourglass (adds 5 extra moves). Power-ups don't cost a move to use. You can earn them through gameplay, daily rewards, or purchase them with coins.
What are stars for?
Stars are earned by completing levels. You can earn 1, 2, or 3 stars per level depending on your score. Stars are used to renovate Baba's dream house — upgrade the living room, kitchen, bedroom, and more!
I'm stuck on a level. Any tips?
Focus on making bigger chains (5+) to create special pieces — they're much more powerful than small chains. Look for L-shaped and T-shaped patterns to create Fez Bombs. Save your power-ups for the toughest moments. And don't forget you can watch a rewarded ad for 5 extra moves when you run out!
Is the game free?
Yes! Blastic Bubbles is free to download and play. The game contains ads and optional in-app purchases (Remove Ads, coin packs). You can enjoy the full game without spending anything.
What devices are supported?
Blastic Bubbles is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices. Requires iOS 15.0 or later, or Android 7.0 or later.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us at burakdumer22@hotmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots are very helpful!
