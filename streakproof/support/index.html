FAQ

How do streak freezes work?

If you miss a scheduled day, a freeze is automatically used so your streak isn't reset. Free users get a limited number of freezes each month; you can earn an extra freeze by watching a short ad, or get unlimited freezes with Pro.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore purchases", or reinstall and sign in with the same Apple ID.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. See our Privacy Policy.
