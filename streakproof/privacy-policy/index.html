Privacy Policy — Streakproof

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Streakproof ("the app", "we") handles your information. Streakproof is operated by Burak Dümer.

1. Data stored on your device

Your habits, completions, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not require an account and we do not upload this data to our own servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Streakproof shows ads via Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may collect and process device and usage information (such as advertising identifiers). Where required (e.g. in the EEA/UK), we present a consent choice via Google's User Messaging Platform before personalized ads are requested. On iOS, we ask for App Tracking Transparency permission. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data.

3. Purchases (RevenueCat)

If you buy a subscription, purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user identifier and purchase information to validate and restore your subscription. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy. We do not receive your payment card details; those are handled by Apple.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not collect your name, email, or contacts.

5. Children

Streakproof is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR in the EU, KVKK in Türkiye), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because habit data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting a habit or uninstalling the app. For ad/purchase data held by Google/RevenueCat, please refer to their policies linked above.

7. Contact

Questions? Email burakdumer22@hotmail.com.
