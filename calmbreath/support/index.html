FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases", or reinstall and sign in with the same Apple ID. CalmBreath Pro is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID, so there is nothing to renew or cancel.

How do I delete my data?

All of your data stays on your device. You can erase it at any time from Settings → Reset in the app, or by deleting the app. We hold no data about you on any server. See our Privacy Policy.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. CalmBreath has no account, no ads, and no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.
