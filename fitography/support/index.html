SUPPORT

Got questions about Fitography? Here are answers to the most common ones. If you need more help, reach out below.

How does the AI create my workout plan?
During onboarding, you share your goals, equipment, schedule, and any physical limitations. Our AI uses this information to generate a personalized 14-day workout program. Every 2 weeks, it analyzes your training data and creates a smarter plan based on your progress.
I'm a complete beginner. Is this app for me?
Absolutely — Fitography was built specifically for people who are new to fitness. Every exercise comes with clear, plain-language instructions. The AI adapts to your level and never pushes you too hard too fast.
How do I restore my subscription?
Open Fitography → Profile tab → tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any active subscription tied to your Apple ID. If you're still having trouble, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Can I use Fitography without gym equipment?
Yes! During onboarding, select "No equipment" (bodyweight only). The AI will generate a complete plan using only bodyweight exercises. You can update your equipment later if your situation changes.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Fitography → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund from Apple directly.
My plan feels too easy / too hard. What should I do?
After each workout, rate the difficulty (1-5). The AI uses this feedback to adjust your next plan cycle. You can also manually regenerate your plan from Profile → Re-generate Plan. The more you train and provide feedback, the better the AI gets at calibrating your workouts.
Does Fitography work offline?
Yes — your current plan and cached exercise demos are available offline, so you can work out at the gym without internet. Workout logging also works offline and syncs when you're back online. Plan generation requires an internet connection.
