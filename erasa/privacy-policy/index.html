Privacy Policy — Erasa

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Erasa ("the app", "we") handles your information. Erasa is an on-device photo object eraser operated by Burak Dümer.

1. Your photos stay on your device

Erasa edits your photos entirely on your device, using on-device machine learning. Your photos are never uploaded to us or to anyone else, and the app works fully offline (you can use it in airplane mode). There is no account and no sign-in. When you save an edited photo, it is written back to your own photo library, and location (GPS) metadata is removed by default.

2. No ads, no tracking

Erasa shows no advertising and contains no advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not collect an advertising identifier and we do not track you across apps or websites.

3. Purchases

Erasa is free to use. If an optional one-time "Pro" upgrade is offered in a future version, purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app-user identifier and purchase information solely to validate and restore your purchase. We never receive your payment-card details — those are handled by Apple. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. What we do not do

We do not upload your photos. We do not sell your personal data. We do not collect your name, email, or contacts, and we run no identifying analytics.

5. Children

Erasa is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR in the EU/UK, KVKK in Türkiye), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because your photos and edits live on your device, you control them directly — delete them in Photos or uninstall the app at any time. For any purchase data held by Apple/RevenueCat, please refer to their policies.
