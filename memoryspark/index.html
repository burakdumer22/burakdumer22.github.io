HOW IT WORKS

Three taps to magic.

01
Grant photo access
Memory Spark scans your library on-device using face detection to find photos with people. Your photos never leave your phone.
02
Tap "Surprise Me" or Craft Your Own
Surprise Me picks 10 random photos and a random art style — one tap, pure surprise. Craft Your Own lets you shuffle photos and choose from 10 beautiful styles.
03
AI creates your memory art
The AI describes your photos, blends all the scenes into one prompt, and generates a unique artwork capturing the essence of those moments. Save it, share it, frame it.
